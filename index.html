Welcome to Pinball!


1. this game is a combination of a pinball game and a question game.

2. Answer questions correctly to score points while playing pinball!

3. Choose your difficulty level at the start of questions.

4. Select the correct answer from the options provided using the buttons below.

5. If the wrong answer is selected, the chance of playing the pinball game is lost and unable to score points.

6. if the correct answer is selected, you will be able to play the pinball game and score points.

7. Higher difficulty levels will give more points when you answer correctly.

8. To start playing the pinball game, hold the down arrow key to gain power and release it to launch the ball.

9. Use the left and right arrow keys to control the flippers and keep the ball in play.


Hit "Play Game" to start or hit "History" to view your game history.

Enjoy the challenge and aim for the highest score. Good luck and have fun!
